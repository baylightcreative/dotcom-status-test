How to: Troubleshoot

  1. Identify if the issue is local to your office internet using www.speedtest.net
  2. Try using a different internet browser like Chrome or Firefox
  3. Update your browser and Adobe Flash
  4. Clear your browser cache

How to: Prepare to work offline

  1. Print the next day's schedule
  2. Complete pending Rx refill requests the day before
  3. Have a plan to document your patient visits
  4. Plan alternatives for accessing labs and prescriptions

How to: Avoid local disruptions

  1. Have a prepaid wireless 3G hub or other back-up internet
  2. Use surge protectors and laptops with good batteries
  3. Identify a second location where you can access internet
